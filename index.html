Bio

Dragons Delight began in 2014, as owner Saima Awan started baking cakes from her new home in Amsterdam. In 2015, Dragons Delight found its lair in the Tolstraat, and opened for breakfast and brunch in July 2015. Joined by hospitality veteran Jade Ashworth in 2017, they have taken Dragons Delight to the next level. The lair is now showcasing both seasonal menus as well as new and established artists- the mantra of Dragons is to never stop creating and growing.

Cute cafe, Great menu!

I was made to feel very welcome and was served very promptly and curtiously. The food was delicious, so much to choose from on the menu but the eggs benedict was fabulous. So were the green smoothie and the fresh ginger tea. They have pie on the menu which had it been later in the day I would probably have gone for. Home-made food, good prices, great service- what's not to love?

Amazing!

Great portion size, friendly staff and the food was amazing!!! I’m going to make it a tradition to visit this restaurant every time I visit Amsterdam.

Fab Little Find, Next to our hotel!

Fab little find. Our hotel was charging 21 euro for brekkie, which we felt was a little steep. Went over the road to this cafe and we were not disappointed. Brilliant service, brilliant food. Not an egg lover, but the scrambled eggs were the best I’ve had. And the cappuccino was to die for. Reasonably priced and served with a smile.

Delicious breakfast

I ordered the chocolate pancakes and they were cooked quickly, served hot. Incredibly friendly and good service!

Hipster and Amazing food!

Definately a hipster-indie brunch restaurant. The staff were so friendly and helpful and the food was just simply amazing!!! We came as a family and we were shocked at the quality of food. I had the English breakfast which was superb and the rest of my family had pancakes. If you’re ever there order the Berry Bomb smoothie! The waiting time was abit unrealisable a times and slow but eventually we got our food and were not disappointed. For a filling breakfast for four it only cost us around £64. Very pleased and will return!

Find Us

You can find us at:

Tolstraat 200, Amsterdam 1074 VN, The Netherlands.

To get here from Amsterdam Centraal:

You can take Tram 3 or 4 to Van Woustraat, and turn left down Tolstraat and walk to the end.

Alternative Transport Methods:

Amsterdam is of course the city of bikes, so you could also cycle to us with a trusty satnav system and lock your bikes up outside. Uber and Viavan also operate in the city but you will need their apps to book one!
